The Process
01 Data & Intake Pipeline data, win/loss patterns, existing decks, LP letters, website copy. We build the full picture before asking a single question.
02 Stakeholder Interviews 30–45 min conversations across your full ecosystem: GPs, operating partners, portfolio founders, and LPs. We listen for what you do that no other fund can honestly claim.
03 Synthesis Workshop We present 4–6 positioning territories, each ranked by differentiation strength and messaging risk. You make the strategic choice — we make it clear.
04 Delivery Production-ready outputs aligned to one clear point of view — positioning deck, LP narrative, and founder messaging.
