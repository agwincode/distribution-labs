# Distribution Labs: Offerings & Mission

Distribution Labs is an AI-enabled marketing advisory firm focused on Series A through C tech founders. Our goal is to support founders in telling their stories and building marketing engines that help them outpace the competition.

## Mission & Core Values
- **Target Audience:** Series A through C tech founders.
- **Brand Feeling:** Different from a traditional marketing advisory perspective, expert, smart, good partners, and supportive of founders in telling their story.
- **Goal:** Help founders outpace competition through superior storytelling and marketing engines.

## Product Offerings

### 1. Founder Storytelling
Build the founder's real narrative — for press, podcasts, investors, and hiring. Includes PR strategy for the channels that matter now.

### 2. Positioning & Differentiation
Figure out what actually makes you different. Deliver a positioning deck, sales deck storyline, and homepage revamp built on customer evidence.

### 3. Growth Engine
Diagnose why you're not growing faster. Deliver a 90-day roadmap, channel-by-channel recommendations, and a next-hire plan.

### 4. AI Transformation
Level up your marketing org with real AI workflows — by team, by function, not point solutions.
